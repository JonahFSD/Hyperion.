## No Signal Across Industries

Cosine similarity among pairs from different SIC codes has zero predictive power.

**Numbers:**
- Cross-SIC Spearman rho: 0.016

**Kills:** H05 (SAE does not capture cross-industry signal), triggers D03 (cross-SIC is stretch goal, not core).

**Implication:** Hyperion's core use case is within-industry retrieval. Cross-industry analog-finding requires different signal.
