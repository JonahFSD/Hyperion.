## Cluster Size Divergence Over Time

SAE cluster sizes are shrinking while SIC/SBERT are growing.

**Numbers:**
- SAE: 2.16 (2010) → 1.48 (2020)
- SIC: 2.71 → 4.34
- SBERT: 14.1 → 29.1

**Kills:** H04 (cluster size distribution not comparable), supports E04 finding.

**Implication:** SAE mechanical advantage from small clusters; need size-stratified or pair-weighted metrics.
