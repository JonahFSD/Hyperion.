## Signal Exists Within Industries

Among pairs from same SIC code, cosine predicts return correlation much better.

**Numbers:**
- Within-SIC Spearman rho: 0.117 (mean across years)
- Grows to 0.212 by 2020

**Supports:** H01, H05 (within-industry retrieval is viable)

**Key:** 5x stronger than global rho (0.022), suggests filtering by industry unleashes signal.

**Implication:** T04 (within-SIC precision) is critical path test.
