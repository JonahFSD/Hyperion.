## SAE Advantage Statistically Significant

SAE's MC advantage over SIC and SBERT is highly significant.

**Numbers:**
- All t-statistics > 5.5
- All p_BY (Benjamini-Hochberg corrected) < 0.001

**Supports:** H01, but does NOT prove signal (confounded by cluster size, market regime).
