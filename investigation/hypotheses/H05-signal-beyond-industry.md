## SAE Captures Signal Beyond Industry

SAE captures similarity signal beyond industry membership.

**Evidence:**
- Within-SIC: rho=0.117 (real signal, growing to 0.212 by 2020)
- Cross-SIC: rho=0.016 (essentially zero)

**Implication:**
SAE useful WITHIN industries but adds nothing across industries. Limits use case but doesn't kill it.

**Consequence:** Focus retrieval on within-industry scenarios (D03).
